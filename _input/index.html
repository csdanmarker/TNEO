Our Mission

We enhance the economy of Northeast Ohio by raising the profile of the region and collaborating with our partners and others to attract new businesses and help those that are here to grow. We help bring together local, regional and state resources to make the case for investment in Northeast Ohio, focusing on proven businesses that will contribute to the region's success. We believe in the unlimited potential of Northeast Ohio.
